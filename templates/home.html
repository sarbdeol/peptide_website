Is a Certificate of Analysis provided?
Yes. Every peptide order includes a batch-specific COA with HPLC chromatogram, mass spectrometry data, and purity analysis. Additional testing documentation available upon request.
What are the storage requirements?
Lyophilized peptides should be stored at below 32 degrees F for one year. Once reconstituted, store at Refrigereted up to 30 days for short-term use . Detailed storage instructions included with each product.
Do you provide dosing or protocol guidance?
No. All peptides are sold strictly for in-vitro research purposes. We do not provide dosing recommendations, protocols for human use, or any medical advice. Researchers must follow appropriate laboratory safety procedures.
What is your shipping policy?
Orders are shipped with temperature-controlled packaging to maintain peptide stability. Tracking information provided for all orders. Shipping times vary by destination. All packages are discreetly labeled.
Who can order research peptides?
Research peptides are available to qualified laboratories, research institutions, and licensed researchers. All customers must be 18+ years of age and acknowledge that products are for research use only.
